How to prevent/counter Climate Change


How we can make a change

Using renewable energy is one of the best ways you can help. Solar panals and wind enery are common ways people are trying to help.
Public Transport is the probaly the most efficent way of Combating Climate change. By using a bus or train insed of a car you preserve fossil fuels from being used, and in turn help stop Climate change.
This one may sound simple but can make a huge effect over time.Turn off the tap when you're not using it. This will save huge amounts over time and prevent large amounts of wasted material.
This one is as simple as the previous. Turn off electronics when they're not being used and pull the plugs on them at night. Sleep mode still wastes energy and by pulling the plug you save even more.
Speak out. By speaking to others and informing them what to do and why to help prevent Climate Change you will be making a huge difference to not only their carbon footprint but your own.
